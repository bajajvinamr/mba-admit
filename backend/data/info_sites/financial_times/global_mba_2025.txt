Instead, we are publishing directories of accredited executive course providers. The directories allow users to search and examine different data points including school name, location and revenue range. Business schools are listed alphabetically by name. An interactive version of the Custom table is available at ft.com/custom-education/list/2021 and the Open listing at ft.com/open-education/list/2021. These are listings, not rankings, of programmes offered by schools accredited by AACSB and/or Equis. † No data given.
