Information Sessions Stanford GSB Admissions Gears Up to Host East Coast Information Sessions Published: June 21, 2015 by Clear Admit Are you considering Stanford Graduate School of Business (GSB) but unable to head to Palo Alto now to see the campus first hand? We’ve got good news for prospective applicants on the East Coast: Members of the Stanford GSB admissions team will be joined by Stanford MBA alumni for a series of events from Washington, DC to Boston between now and September. Get an overview of the MBA program from admissions representatives, hear about student life from those who have experienced it and ask any questions you have about the school or its admissions process. On tap are both general information sessions and sessions designed specifically for female prospective applicants and for prospective applicants of color. Advanced registration is required—sign up now to reserve your spot. Also, if you’re considering Stanford GSB as one of your target schools, don’t miss Clear Admit’s Stanford GSB School Profile , which provides an overview of the academic curriculum and learning environment, active student clubs, annual events, recruiting activity, placement statistics and more. This useful guide brings a ton of valuable information about Stanford GSB together in one place to help streamline your school research process.
